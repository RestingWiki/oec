Welcome to OISP English Club

What is OEC?

OISP English Club (OEC) is the sole English club under Office for Education Programs (OISP) at Vietnam National University - Ho Chi Minh city University of Technology.

Established in 2017, OEC was created with the goal of providing a friendly and beneficial environment for students who are deeply passionate about the English language. With the slogan “Never give up - OEC speaks up”, not only does OEC aim to build a friendly environment, but also organizes academic competitions to spread English into all aspects of life, overcoming the language barrier for students at VNU-HCMUT.

OEC Nobel Events

  • Summer Debate Battle

    This event, held within the Summer Green framework, gathered elementary and middle school students in Tan Thoi Nhat ward, District 12. The competition helped kids sharpen their critical thinking and English skills through debates on daily life topics.

  • IELTS Workshop

    An annual workshop revolving around the four skills of Speaking, Writing, Listening, and Reading in the IELTS test. It aims to provide students with knowledge, answer their questions, and boost their confidence in conquering their desired IELTS band score.

OEC Weekly Event & Bonding Time

  • Conversation Room

    An activity aimed at improving speaking skills. A group of 3-5 people, led by a host, share their views on various topics.

  • The Musical Night

    A music exchange program for club members.

OEC participated events

  • OISP International Festival

    An annual cultural exchange event organized to promote and integrate the cultural identity of nations to the youth. OEC successfully spread British culture in OIF 2023.

  • OISP Open Day

    A large annual career guidance, admissions, and career orientation event organized by the International Training Office of Bach Khoa University - VNU - HCM City for parents and candidates interested in international training programs. Here, the OISP English Club supported the English Office in student admissions counseling.
